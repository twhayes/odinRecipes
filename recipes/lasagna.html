Lasagna

Description

This is a wonderful recipe to make quickly for a nice family dinner. The ingredients are common and generally found in any kitchen, which makes it easy to prepare, even on short notice.

Note that this can easily be converted into a vegan recipe by substituting cauliflower rice for the ground beef.

  • Ground Beef
  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  1. Boil pasta for 2 minutes
  2. Saute ground beef
  3. Layer pasta, ground beef, cheese, and tomato sauce in a baking dish
  4. Bake at 350 for 45 minutes or until top layer of pasta is brown
